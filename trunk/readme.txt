Provide your WordPress with a chatbot as contact point.

== Description ==

Bring your WordPress to a higher level by replacing the traditional contact form with a chatbot.

When your customers want to contact you, they chat with your chatbot instead of fill out a form.

They will impressed with the unique conversational experience.

You'll then receive an email with the following information about the customer.

* Email address
* Name
* City and country
* The reason to contact

It's cool.

== Installation ==

Just follow the standard WordPress plugin installation steps.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
1. Provide your email address.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I teach the chatbot to answer questions? =

No. Contact chatbot is designed to replace your contact form and you cannot teach it.

If you want your chatbot to answer customers questions about your business, check out https://wordpress.org/plugins/acobot-chatbot/.
